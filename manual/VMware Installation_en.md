  <strong> VMware Installation Guide </strong>
  <br>
</h1>

<p align="center">
  Languages:
  <a href="./VMware%20Installation.md"> 简体中文 </a> ·
  <a href="./VMware%20Installation_en.md"> English </a>
</p>

1. **Launch the Installer**  
   Double-click the installation package to start the VMware installer, then click `Next` to proceed to the next step, as shown below:
<p align="center">
  <img src="../image/VMware%20Installation%20diagram/P1.png" width="60%">
</p>

2. **Accept the License Agreement**  
   Check the box labeled `I accept the terms in the License Agreement` to agree to the software license, then click `Next` to continue, as shown below:
<p align="center">
  <img src="../image/VMware%20Installation%20diagram/P2.png" width="60%">
</p>

3. **Compatibility Settings**  
   Ignore the prompt indicating that *Hyper-V is not detected on the host*, and click `Next` to continue, as shown below:
<p align="center">
  <img src="../image/VMware%20Installation%20diagram/P3.png" width="60%">
</p>

4. **Select Installation Location**  
   You may keep the default installation path or change it according to your needs. After selecting the location, click `Next` to proceed, as shown below:
<p align="center">
  <img src="../image/VMware%20Installation%20diagram/P4.png" width="60%">
</p>

5. **User Experience Settings**  
   Uncheck the first option if desired, or adjust the settings based on your preference. The first option checks for updates at startup, and the second option joins the Customer Experience Improvement Program. Click `Next` to continue, as shown below:
<p align="center">
  <img src="../image/VMware%20Installation%20diagram/P5.png" width="60%">
</p>

6. **Shortcut Settings**  
   By default, both options are selected. You may modify them as needed. The first option creates a desktop shortcut, and the second adds an entry to the Start Menu. Click `Next` to continue, as shown below:
<p align="center">
  <img src="../image/VMware%20Installation%20diagram/P6.png" width="60%">
</p>

7. **Confirm Installation**  
   Click `Install` to begin the installation process. Alternatively, click `Back` to modify previous settings or `Cancel` to abort the installation, as shown below:
<p align="center">
  <img src="../image/VMware%20Installation%20diagram/P7.png" width="60%">
</p>

8. **Exit the Installation Wizard**  
   Click `Finish` to exit the installation wizard and complete the installation, as shown below:
<p align="center">
  <img src="../image/VMware%20Installation%20diagram/P8.png" width="60%">
</p>
